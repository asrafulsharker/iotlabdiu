Our Innovative IOT LAB DIU

The impact of IoT (Internet of Things) in daily life is constantly increasing. In view of the importance of this, IoT Lab has been set up at Daffodil International University with the aim of educating the students in academic and practical education. This lab has facilities for research or projects through teamwork along with group study.

Our students, faculty and alumni are widely recognized as being among the best in the field, winning prestigious research fellowships and paper awards at all of the top venues. Moreover, our work has been featured in numerous local and national media outlets. Learn more about who we are behind the team and what we do in this exciting research environment!

About Us

The IoT Lab is one of the specialized labs at Daffodil University. Here we are committed to providing students with up-to-date ideas about IoT. There are facilities for projects and research in a pleasant environment with adequate materials.

Rules & Regulations

However, it is important to follow some rules and regulations while using this lab.
Students will not be allowed in the laboratory without an ID card with DIU Ribbon.
Unnecessary and loud conversation must be avoided with others during laboratory classes.
Proper uses of the instruments and equipment must be learnt.
In case of damage/broken down of any instruments by a student, he/she must be identified and will be penalized.
Equipment must be taken care off for long time use.
All equipment must be returned to the Technical Officer of the Lab after use.
Without permission of the Adviser/Course Teacher/Technical Officer, students are not allowed to work in the Lab.
Never open or remove cover of the equipment in the laboratory without authorization of the Adviser/Lab Teacher/Technical Officer.
Do not displace or remove laboratory equipment without permission and authorization of the Adviser/Lab Teacher/Technical Officer.
Use gloves, mask in the Lab. (Where applicable)
Must be careful about outfit while using heat/fire in the Lab.
Drinking or eating something is forbidden in the Lab.
Must be cautions while using electric power in Lab.
Tools having non-conductive cover must be used for electrical work.
Ornaments and Jewelries, including rings, must be put off because these may encounter expose circuits.
Keep the body or any part of it, out of the circuit, where interconnecting wire and cables are involved, they should be arranged so people will not trip over them.
Switch off the supply if you change the experiment even when voltage is low.
After the Lab session, switch off every supply, disconnect and disintegrate the experiment.
When disassembling the circuit, first remove the source of power.
Before leaving the lab, you must shut down your used computer at your own risk.
The cleanliness of the lab must be maintained. Any kind of waste should be thrown in the bin.
Learn and know what to do in any emergency. Be familiar with the locations and operation of safety and emergency equipment such as emergency power OFF in the Lab.
Report off any broken plug/exposed wires/damages to equipment to the Adviser/ Teacher/ Technical Officer.
